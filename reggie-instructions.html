Entry Instructions

This year the Knickerbocker is using Reggie to handle contest entries, judging, flight assignment, and all of the details of running a contest. This may be new to you, but hundreds of contests are now using Reggie so it is likely not the last time you will see it!

Reggie is an unusual website, not really up-to-date with modern sites. It's not hard to use on a regular computer, but can be a little tricky on tablets or phones where you must scroll through long pop-up lists. If you're having trouble on your tablet, try again from a computer. We apologize for the inconvenience!

Here's the process:

  1. Go to the Reggie entry screen for the competition at http://reggiebeer.com/ReggieEntry.php?CompetitionID=ZEUFQP1000173.
  2. If you already have an account in Reggie (i.e., you've entered a competition hosted by Reggie before), just log in. If not, click on Create Account, fill in the info, and click "Create". Include your homebrew club if it is listed, or choose "Unlisted" if you can't find your club.
  3. Once logged in to Reggie, click on "Entries". Then click on "Add An Entry".
  4. Pick the beer style. Depending on the style you choose, some options may become available, for example to specify a fruit additive. Several optoinal fields are available, too -- you can upload your recipe, give the name of the beer, and name your co-brewers. Once you've filled in the details for the entry, click "Save New Entry". Repeat for any additional entries.
  5. Select where you will be dropping off your entry. We have a lot of drop-off locations, and their full addresses are listed on the entry details page for your convenience.
  6. When you have everything finished, check the boxes for the new entries and click "Print Bottle Labels" and print out the resulting PDF. This has labels for each bottle that you can cut out and rubber-band to the bottles.
  7. Finally, unless you intend to include a check with your entries, use the PayPal form at the bottom of the page to pay for your entry.

If you have any questions about the entry process, please contact the contest organizer at djmitche@gmail.com.
